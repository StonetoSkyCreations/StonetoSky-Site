Design, art & song rooted in the living world.

Stone to Sky is the home of Brooke’s branding, art, and song – where design, illustration, and voice come together in service to beauty, depth, and the more-than-human world.

I support people, projects, and places that are tending to culture, community, and the Earth.

Work with me

About Brooke

Brooke is a designer, artist, and singer who moves between worlds – digital and hand-drawn, inner and outer, personal and collective.

Her work weaves three main strands:

  • Branding & design for soul-led projects and small businesses
  • Artwork & illustration that honour the seasons, the body, and the Earth
  • Singing & facilitation in circles, ceremonies, and community spaces

Stone to Sky is the meeting point of these threads: a place for clear, grounded design that still remembers we belong to something sacred and alive.

Branding & Design

I create visual identities and design assets for people and projects that care about beauty, integrity, and the Earth.

What I offer:

  • Brand discovery and visual direction
  • Logos and wordmarks
  • Colour palettes and type systems
  • Layout and design for posters, calendars, and print pieces
  • Simple websites and sales pages (in collaboration where needed)

Ideal for: Herbalists, artists, healers, retreat spaces, small businesses, and community projects that want to feel visually coherent and true to themselves.

Enquire about branding »

Artwork & Illustration

My artwork is an ongoing conversation with the seasons, the body, and the subtle world.

I create:

  • Original pieces and illustrated collections
  • Seasonal calendars and lunar maps
  • Botanical and symbolic illustrations
  • Custom pieces for homes, books, and spaces

If you’d like to carry my work in your shop, or commission a piece for your space or project, I’d love to hear from you.

Enquire about artwork »

Singing & Facilitation

My voice work lives in circles, ceremonies, and small gatherings where people come to soften, listen, and reconnect.

I offer:

  • Singing and sound in ceremony and ritual spaces
  • Co-facilitation of retreats and workshops
  • Gentle guided practices that weave song, presence, and grounding

This work is intimate and relational. If you’re holding a space and feel my presence and voice would support the field, reach out and we can feel into it together.

Enquire about singing & facilitation »

Co-Creative Projects

Some of the most alive work happens in the overlap – where design, art, and song come together around a shared intention.

I’m open to collaboration on:

  • Visual worlds and storytelling for creative projects
  • Offerings that blend artwork, song, and ritual
  • Seasonal and Earth-based projects that need both design and facilitation
  • Quiet, long-term creative partnerships with aligned people and places

If you feel a resonance but don’t see a neat box for it here, you’re welcome to write and share the seed of your idea.
