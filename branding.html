Process

How we’ll work together

Discovery & alignment

We meet to understand your story, audience, and needs. From there, we set a rhythm and milestones.

  • Clarify your offer and voice
  • Agree on scope and timelines
  • Shared folders for smooth collaboration
Design & refinement

Concepts flow into logos, palettes, and layouts. We iterate together until it feels like a full-body yes.

  • Illustration and typography choices
