Highlights & collections

Seasonal calendars & lunar maps

Layered illustrations that track the rhythms of the moon, tides, and seasonal shifts.

  • Lunar and seasonal calendar suites created annually
  • Mix of hand-drawn ink, watercolour, and digital finishing
  • Print-ready layouts for wall calendars and posters

Botanical & body studies

Detailed drawings that weave plants, anatomy, and subtle symbolism.

  • Medicinal botanicals with accompanying notes and sigils
  • Anatomical line work honouring the breath, bones, and nervous system
  • Works sized for prints, book interiors, and educational material

Ceremony & sacred space

Pieces created for gatherings, retreats, and devotional spaces.

  • Altarpiece illustrations for seasonal rituals
  • Custom graphics for event posters and invitations
  • Symbolic maps and visual aids for guided journeys
