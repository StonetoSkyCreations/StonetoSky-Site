How commissions work

Share the brief

Tell me about the feeling, story, and format you’re after. We’ll confirm the size, medium, and any key symbols.

Sketch & refine

I create sketches for your review. You’ll have two rounds of refinements before we finalise the artwork.

Deliver & frame

High-quality files (and originals where relevant) are delivered with notes on printing, paper stocks, and care.

Need illustration plus design? I can pair artwork with layout design for booklets, posters, or packaging.

Ready to create?

Share the story or space you’re making art for and I’ll suggest mediums, sizes, and timelines that fit.
